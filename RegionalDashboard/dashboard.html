Key messages

Regional mean temperature for WMO RA 1 Africa

The year 2021 was ranked between the 3rd and 5th highest on record. The mean value for 2021 was 0.71°C relative to the 1981-2010 average (0.55-0.88°C depending on the data set used). 5 data sets were used in this assessment: Berkeley Earth, ERA5, GISTEMP, HadCRUT5, and NOAAGlobalTemp. Relative to a 1961-1990 baseline, the mean value for 2021 was 1.14°C (1.05-1.23°C depending on the data set used). 4 data sets were used in the assessment relative to 1961-1990.
