Key messages

Regional mean temperature for WMO RA 1 Africa

The year 2022 was ranked between the 9th and 16th highest on record. The mean value for 2022 was 0.16°C relative to the 1991-2020 average (0.06-0.28°C depending on the data set used). 6 data sets were used in this assessment: Berkeley Earth, ERA5, GISTEMP, HadCRUT5, JRA-55, and NOAAGlobalTemp. Relative to a 1961-1990 baseline, the mean value for 2022 was 0.84°C (0.74-0.91°C depending on the data set used). 5 data sets were used in the assessment relative to 1961-1990.
