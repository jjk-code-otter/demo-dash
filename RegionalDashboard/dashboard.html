Key messages

Global mean temperature

The year 2023 was ranked the 1st warmest on record. The mean value for 2023 was 0.56°C relative to the 1991-2020 average (0.51-0.60°C depending on the data set used). 7 data sets were used in this assessment: Berkeley Earth, ERA5, GISTEMP, HadCRUT5, JRA-55, Kadow et al., and NOAAGlobalTemp. Relative to a 1961-1990 baseline, the mean value for 2023 was 1.08°C (1.04-1.12°C depending on the data set used). 7 data sets were used in the assessment relative to 1961-1990.
