Key messages

Regional mean temperature for WMO RA 6 Europe

The year 2021 was ranked between the 6th and 10th highest on record. The mean value for 2021 was 0.90°C (0.76-0.99°C depending on the data set used). 5 data sets were used in this assessment: Berkeley Earth, ERA5, GISTEMP, HadCRUT5, and NOAAGlobalTemp.
