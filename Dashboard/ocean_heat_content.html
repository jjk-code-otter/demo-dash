Key messages

Global ocean heat content

The year 2021 was ranked the 1st highest on record. The mean value for 2021 was 15.37zJ (14.79-16.12zJ depending on the data set used). 3 data sets were used in this assessment: Cheng et al, Ishii, and Levitus.
