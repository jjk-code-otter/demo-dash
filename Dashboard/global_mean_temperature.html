Key messages

Global mean temperature

The year 2021 was ranked between the 5th and 7th highest on record. The mean value for 2021 was 1.11°C relative to the 1850-1900 average (1.08-1.16°C depending on the data set used). 6 data sets were used in this assessment: Berkeley Earth, ERA5, GISTEMP, HadCRUT5, JRA-55, and NOAAGlobalTemp.

Global mean sea-surface temperature

The year 2021 was ranked between the 7th and 8th highest on record. The mean value for 2021 was 0.31°C relative to the 1981-2010 average (0.29-0.34°C depending on the data set used). 2 data sets were used in this assessment: ERSST and HadSST4.

Global mean land temperature

The year 2021 was ranked between the 6th and 7th highest on record. The mean value for 2021 was 0.64°C relative to the 1981-2010 average (0.60-0.69°C depending on the data set used). 3 data sets were used in this assessment: Berkeley Earth, CRUTEM5, and GHCNv4.
