Key messages

Mountain glacier cumulative mass balance

This was the 34th consecutive year of negative mass balance since 1988. Cumulative glacier loss since 1970 is -25.5m w.e..

Greenland ice sheet cumulative mass balance

There are 2 data sets of Greenland mass balance. In the NASA GRACE data set, the mass change between 1 September 2020 and 31 August 2021 was -302.61Gt, which is a smaller loss than the average for 2005-2020 of -325.05Gt. In the Mankoff et al. 2021 data set, the mass change between 1 September 2020 and 31 August 2021 was -198.06Gt, which is a smaller loss than the average for 2005-2020 of -238.60Gt.
