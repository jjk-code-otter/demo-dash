Key messages

Arctic sea-ice extent

Arctic sea ice extent in March 2021 was between -0.77 and -0.74million km2. This was between the 10th and 11th lowest extent on record. In September the extent was between -1.46 and -1.31million km2. This was the 12th lowest extent on record. Data sets used were: NSIDC and OSI SAF
