Key messages

Atmospheric concentration of carbon dioxide

The monthly value for November 2021 was the 1st highest on record at 413.2ppm.

Atmospheric concentration of methane

The monthly value for September 2021 was the 1st highest on record at 1860.0ppb.

Atmospheric concentration of nitrous oxide

The monthly value for December 2021 was the 1st highest on record at 335.1ppb.
